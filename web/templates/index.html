The concept

What is +EV betting?

Every sportsbook builds a margin — the "vig" — into their odds. It's how they profit regardless of outcomes. But books aren't perfect. They sometimes price teams slightly off from reality, especially in less-watched markets.

A +EV (positive expected value) bet is one where the book's implied probability is lower than the true probability of that outcome. Bet enough of these and the math works in your favor over time — just like a casino's edge works for them.

We remove the vig from the sharpest books to find true probability, then compare it to every other book's line to find where the edge lives.
