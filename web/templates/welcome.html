What is +EV betting?

Every sportsbook buries a commission (the "vig") into their odds. We strip that out to find the true probability of each outcome. When the true probability of winning is higher than what the odds imply, that's a +EV bet — a mathematical edge that wins you money long-term.

{# Step 2 #}
2

Reading a bet card

Each card tells you exactly what to bet and where. Here's a sample:

Example card
Over 6.5  ·  NHL Total +7.2% EV
FanDuel  ·  -115 odds True prob: 54.8%

The EV% is your edge. Anything above 3% is flagged. Higher is better.

Start with one league and one market type until you're confident, then expand.

{# Step 4 #}
4

½ Kelly staking

Each card shows a ½ Kelly percentage — a conservative, math-backed stake size relative to your bankroll. If your bankroll is $1,000 and Kelly shows 2.5%, bet $25.

This keeps you in the game during variance while maximizing long-run growth. Never bet more than Kelly suggests.
