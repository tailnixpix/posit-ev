🔮 What are Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets like Kalshi, Polymarket, NoVig, and ProphetX are exchange-style platforms where users trade on the probability of outcomes — with little to no vig (house edge). Unlike traditional sportsbooks, most prediction markets are available in all 50 US states because they are regulated as financial instruments, not gambling.

Kalshi is federally regulated by the CFTC. Polymarket operates globally. NoVig is a peer-to-peer betting exchange. Because these platforms price outcomes closer to true probability, they often represent the sharpest available line — and can surface edges that traditional sportsbooks miss.
