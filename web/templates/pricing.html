Frequently asked questions

What is expected value (EV) betting?

+EV betting means placing bets where the true probability of winning is higher than what the sportsbook's odds imply. Over a large sample of bets, this results in a mathematical profit — similar to how a casino earns consistently because the math is in their favor.

Which sportsbooks do you compare?

We currently compare odds from DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, and PointsBet. We use the sharpest book as our true probability reference and surface edges at all others.

How is the Kelly criterion used?

Each pick includes a Half-Kelly stake recommendation — a fraction of your total bankroll to wager. We use half-Kelly rather than full-Kelly to reduce variance, which is the industry standard for recreational bettors.

How often are picks updated?

The full pipeline runs every 30 minutes throughout the day. Lines can move quickly when sharp money comes in, so the dashboard shows you the most current edges available.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your subscription is month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel from your account settings and you retain access until the end of the billing period. We process payments through Stripe — your card details are never stored on our servers.

Is this legal?

Posit+EV provides sports betting analysis and information only. Sports betting is legal in many US states and internationally. Always check local laws before placing any wager. We do not take bets or hold funds.
